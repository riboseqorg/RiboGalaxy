About RiboGalaxy

RiboGalaxy provides online tools for analysis of ribo-seq data obtained with ribosome profiling technique see Ingolia et al, 2009.

It serves as a link between raw ribo-seq data and visualisation tools, such as transcriptome browser Trips-viz and genome browser GWIPs-viz.

The RiboGalaxy project is supported in part by a Grant from Science Foundation Ireland under Grant number 18/CRT/6214.

About Galaxy

Galaxy is an open platform for supporting data intensive research. Galaxy is developed by The Galaxy Team with the support of many contributors.

The Galaxy Project is supported in part by NHGRI, NSF, The Huck Institutes of the Life Sciences, The Institute for CyberScience at Penn State, and Johns Hopkins University.
